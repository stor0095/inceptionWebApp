Inception

Inception is a cinematic masterpiece. The movie is about lucid dreams and what the human mind is capable of. Starring Leonardo DiCaprio and many other greats, the movie is complex and really makes you think.

The plot revolves around 'extraction', where the main characters go into the dream world of a subject and try to steal an idea.

The plot thickens when a new challenge arises, instead of stealing an idea, the job is now to plant a seed for a new idea. As result, there is a lot of action in the dream world. A must watch!
